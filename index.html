About Us

We started Pet Toy City from a tiny rental room in Singapore while juggling assignment deadlines as overseas students. Between school, part-time jobs, and homesickness, the one thing that kept us grounded was spending time with rescue pets. We noticed many toys were either overpriced or low quality, so we began sourcing safer, durable toys with student-level budget in mind.

Today, we are still a small founder-run team with the same 留子创业 spirit: work hard, keep learning, and build something meaningful. Every order helps us support local foster communities and create playful days for pets and their humans.
